Control Curve Automater

A rig helper which handles the placement and appearance of control curves. Features include:

  • Automatic naming and placement in scene hierarchy
  • Customizable naming conventions
  • Creates an FK chain with parent constraints automatically if multiple joints are selected at once
  • Orients controls to underlying joint or to a chosen axis
  • Toggleable option to create joint constraints automatically
